About Me

Civil Engineer turned aspiring Software Engineer, fueled by a passion for full-stack development and self-directed learning. My transition from Civil Engineering to Software is driven by a deep love for problem-solving and the logical intricacies of Mathematics, skills that find a natural home in the world of coding.

I'm excited to create impactful solutions and stay at the cutting edge of innovation. Let's connect, learn, and innovate together!
